TITLE : Heart Attack Prediction Application

Overview

The Heart Attack Prediction Application is to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in predicting the risk of heart attacks. Built using machine learning (ML) models, this application leverages historical health data and key clinical indicators to provide accurate and reliable predictions. It offers a user-friendly interface to input patient information and generate risk assessments, aiding in early detection and preventive healthcare measures. The application operates offline, making it accessible in areas with limited internet connectivity and suitable for diverse healthcare settings.

Features

  • Accurate Heart Attack Risk Prediction.
  • User-Friendly Interface.

Conclusion

The Heart Attack Prediction Application provides a powerful tool for healthcare professionals and individuals to assess the risk of heart attacks proactively. By leveraging machine learning technology, the application offers accurate predictions based on patient-specific data, facilitating early intervention and better health outcomes.
